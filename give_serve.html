Ways To Give

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

2 Corinthians 9:7

Electronically

Sending an Interac E-transfer to rccgmessiah@yahoo.com is a secure and verified way to send money to the church.

Cheque

You can as well give by writing a cheque payable to RCCG Messiah.

In Person

Access to a POS terminal and/or an offering envelope provide simple ways to give to the church during service.

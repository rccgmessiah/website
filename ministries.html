Children's Ministry

The Children's Ministry provides childcare for all children above the age of one until the end of elementary school. This ministry introduces what we believe as a church as well as activities to engage the children at an early age. It also organizes an annual Summer Camp, birthday parties, plays and presentations by the children.

See that you do not despise one of these little ones. For I tell you that their angels in heaven always see the face of my Father in heaven.
( Matthew 18:10 )

If you would like to become a member of this ministry, please send an email to rccgmessiah@yahoo.com.

Multimedia Ministry

This team brings expertise and support to the technical systems of the church which includes Sound system and computers. Their task will include taking care of the church audio, video, and IT requirements during regular church services and special programmes held outside of the church, daily support, project work to service and upgrade technical and IT equipments. The team will bring expertise to the computer and network of the church and provide patch application where required.

Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters. ( Colossians 3:23 )

If you would like to become a member of this ministry, please send an email to rccgmessiah@yahoo.com.

Praise & Worship's Ministry

Worship is one of the primary ways that we experience relationship with God and a heartfelt and genuine worship is part of our core values. This Team helps facilitate meaningful and creative worship experience for the congregation during regular church services and special programmes held by the church outside of its regular services.

Women's Ministry

The RCCG Canada WIM is a national Women's Ministry established with a vision to reach out, build, empower and edify women in RCCG and in the surrounding communities where the ministry exists; by so doing these women would be able to fulfill their God-given purpose.This ministry is strategically positioned to be able to utilize and harness all talents, gifts and resources to build up women in the body of Christ.

( Titus 2:3-5 )
